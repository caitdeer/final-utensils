Forks

The best there is, a personal favorite of mine, forks. Universally known as the most American utensil, forks are solid, reliable, and always there for you. The anatomy of a fork

  • Most come with 4 prongs at the end, but if you're super fancy you could get one with 2 or 3!
  • A long smooth stem, some thick and some thin
  • And the best have a great, heavy weight to them!

  • Salads
  • Steak
  • Pasta
  • Scrambled Eggs
  • Roasted vegetbales

Personally, I would give forks a 8/10. They are incredibly dependable, and found at most resturants. Yay forks!

Spoons

This utensil is a league of its own, the spoon dominates the soup field. Perfectly shaped, these little guys are mini bowls just waiting to bring the party to your mouth!

  • A circular, bowl shaped bottom
  • One long smooth stem (just like a fork!)

  • Soup
  • Mac n Cheese
  • Corn Chowder
  • Chili

Spoons put up a good fight, but they aren't a stand out for me. For them, they'll get a 6/10. Better luck next time guys, maybe try to expand your palette a bit more!

Knives

Hellooooo danger! Knives are fierce, pointy, and cut your food with ease (most of the time). When you’re handed a knife you can’t help but get excited for what you’re ‘bout to eat, because I mean if a steak is calling, the knife is coming too.

  • Pointy end (unless its a butter knife!)
  • Serrated or smooth edge
  • Thick handle, with a good weight to it

  • A good old fashioned steak!
  • Fish
  • Fruit
  • Corn on the cob

Now, while I do love a good knife, they are going to get a 4/10 in my book. While they get the job done, they just don't stick out to me.

Chopsticks

Ohhh you know what time it is! Chopsticks are that utensil - they are fun, fresh, and let you know which of your friends don’t have a refined food palette. While they give me hand cramps sometimes, these things are a fun time!

  • Two long, identical sticks
  • One thin end
  • One thicker end

  • Ramen
  • Udon
  • Sushi
  • Dumplings

WOW! Chopsticks are fun, fresh, and a good time... well for most people. I will have to give them a 6/10, just because I know there are better options out there!

Fingers

Yeah, old reliable made the list, fingers are the BOMB! They come with you everywhere, can mimic any utensil, and are used as a utensil in every culture (don’t fact check me please!). They made this list, and a spot in my heart.

Well, I'd imagine that you know what fingers look like. However, I'll give you a quick rundown incase you're not familiar.

  • One palm (flat, rough, somtimes smooth!)
  • Around 5 fingers
  • Fingernails at the end of each finger

  • Hamburger
  • Fries
  • Chicken tenders
  • Pizza

These take the CAKE! Fingers are the best utensil out there, mark my words. 10/10 everytime for these little guys. Wow do they get the job done!
